The Red King (2019)

Photoshop
894x1782 px

This poster was inspired by an anime character called the Red King from the show K Project. I personally loved this character so I wanted to make a piece that exemplified how I felt about the character. This poster is actually the backgorund that I use on my cell phone as well. When creating this piece and many of my other posters, I just tried to make a visually appealing design with cool effects in Photoshop while keeping the different elements of art in mind. Poster design is something that I think I have a natural eye/gift for so I very much enjoy putting my skills into designs for the characters I love.
